How EasyBets Works

EasyBets continuously monitors the Polymarket orderbook and compares it against an AI-driven Fair Value model to find mathematically profitable edges.

Alert Limits & Rules

  • Users are limited to a maximum of 5 active alerts at a time.
  • Alerts trigger automatically when the live Polymarket price reaches your target odds.
  • Once an alert triggers, it is deleted automatically, freeing up your quota.
  • You can manually delete old alerts in the "My Alerts" tab.
